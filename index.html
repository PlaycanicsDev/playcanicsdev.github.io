Made In Alberta: Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 4, 2025

Overview

The Made in Alberta apps prioritize your privacy. We do not collect any personally identifiable information unless explicitly stated and agreed upon. This privacy policy outlines our practices across all our applications and services.

Personal Information

Our apps do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it through support inquiries or feedback forms.

Non-Personal Information

We may collect limited non-personal data such as usage patterns, crash logs, and device information (OS version, screen size, app version) to help us improve performance and user experience.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party analytics or performance monitoring tools that are compliant with industry standards. These services collect only anonymized, aggregated data.

Children's Privacy

Our applications are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe this has occurred, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page. Continued use of the apps implies acceptance of the updated terms.
